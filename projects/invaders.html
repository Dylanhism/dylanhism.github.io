Space Invaders

Space Invaders, a classic game!

During highschool, myself and two others remade space invaders using Python 2 with a package called 'Pygame'. Using our combined efforts, we created every sprite in the game that would look like the original and create our own animations.

The game, like the original had breakable barriers, aliens worth different amounts of points, a life system and most importantly, moving shooting enemies. Once the aliens got far down enough, they speed up making the game more intense. If you killed all the aliens, the game automatically starts up a new round with an extra life for you and your score carrying over.

This project at the time tought me a lot about Python, basic animations, timings and framerates and thinking about how data in programs would be represented appropriately and useful.
